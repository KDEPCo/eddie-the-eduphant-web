How to use the Eddie the AI Discord Bot

Using the Eddie on the Webpage

Accessing the AI Bot:

  • Navigate to the Meet Eddie page on our website.

Provide Eddie with your Questions and Marking Criteria:

  • Type or paste your questions you assesed your students with and their marking criteria into the chat.
  • Eddie will analyse the students answer against the marking criteria and points out trends in the answers.
  • Eddie will provide feedback on how to address these trends and may suggest improvements to assist the teacher on how they can explain the concept.

Creating Lesson Plans:

  • Eddie will ask the teacher if they want a lesson plan based on the feedback they have received or alternatively, type your lesson topic or objectives into the chat.
  • The bot will generate a structured lesson plan, including key points, activities, and learning outcomes.
  • Example: "Generate a lesson plan for Grade 5 Science on the topic of Photosynthesis."

Generating Test Questions:

  • Eddie can generate test questions based on the teachers request or on feedback given.
  • Request questions that test various levels of student understanding.
  • Example: "Generate five multiple-choice questions to test students' understanding of Photosynthesis."

Additional Tips for Improvement

  • Solicit Feedback: Regularly ask for feedback from students to understand which areas need more explanation.
  • Iterate and Adjust: Continuously refine your lesson plans based on student performance and feedback.
  • Professional Development: Engage in professional development opportunities to stay updated on best practices in teaching and lesson planning.
